My Opinion

This movie striked up some controversy. It was put on the back-burner due to Ezra Miller's behavior in the public eye. This movie seemed to be one to end the Ezra Miller Flash, since they had already put so much time, money, and effort into the movie already. For some die-hard Flash fans, this movie was a bust. As for myself, I see this as an opportunity to go a new route with the not only The Flash but the whole DC franchise. The movie was definitely funny, the cameos were nostalgic, but it isn't a movie I would pay to see again.
