My Opinion

I would give this movie a 10/10--it was such a great film. The art style went well with the sound track. The storyline kept you thinking about ways it could coneect to the previous movie or the one ocming next. The character developoment gave you enough information to feel for the characters whether you love them or hate them. The ending also keeps you so excited for the next movie, even though it lasted over two hours. It's a fun movie to watch for any ages.
