My Opinion

I think this movie was good, but a bit slow. The message was great, but there was too much back and forrth between Ember and Wade. The rejection from Ember was becoming too predictable and a bit dreadful. You would just want Ember to give in to Wade's love, but once again he would be rejected. Still, a very heart-warming movie to watch.
